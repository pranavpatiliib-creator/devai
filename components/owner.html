Meet the Owner

Sagar Ambalal Chaudhari

Proprietor

With over 10 years of experience in the industrial electrical sector, Sagar Chaudhari leads Devai Technologies as a trusted distributor and wholesale supplier of electrical products. His leadership focuses on reliability, timely delivery, and building long-term partnerships with businesses.

He holds BTech and MTech degrees in Electrical Engineering, enabling him to combine strong technical understanding with sound commercial judgment. This helps ensure that clients receive the right products, specifications, and solutions suited to their operational needs.

His expertise includes industrial electrical distribution, wholesale supply chain management, vendor coordination, and customer relationship management across Maharashtra. Under his guidance, Devai Technologies operates with transparent practices, consistent quality standards, and a commitment to dependable service.
